OUR FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

Know More

What types of skin and hair conditions does Cosmocol Clinic treat?

We treat a wide range of skin and hair conditions, including but not limited to acne, urticaria, hair fall, unwanted hair, dandruff, psoriasis, skin ulcers, skin allergies, vitiligo, eczema, and more. Our expertise also extends to treating sexually transmitted diseases and leprosy.

Do you offer personalized treatment plans?

Yes, we believe in a personalized approach to care. We take the time to listen to your concerns, understand your unique needs, and develop a tailored treatment plan that works specifically for you.

What kind of technology does Cosmocol Clinic use?

Our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art technology for diagnosis and treatment. We utilize the latest advancements in dermatology and trichology to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Is the clinic environment comfortable for discussing sensitive issues?

Absolutely. We've created a warm, welcoming atmosphere where you can feel at ease discussing your concerns. We understand that skin and hair issues can be sensitive topics, and we ensure a comfortable, confidential environment for all our patients.
